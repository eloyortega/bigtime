About Us

Big Time Protection Inc. (B.T.P) provides security guards for all sites including Commercial Developments, Parking fields, Warehouses & Institutions in New York & New Jersey.

Our Guards

We are fully insured and our guards are licensed. The Guards patrol in vehicle with flashing lights, driving around perimeter. We'll provide guard booths where applicable.

Tracking

We provide our Guards with special equipped phones which is used for tracking and checking into pre-determined sites and additionally giving you access to an app giving location and verification of check-ins in real time while allowing instant communication with Guards on watch.

Assets

Upon request we use audible & non-audible wireless motion sensors and video surveillance, strategically placed at various locations to alert intrusion, all at little or no cost to you.
